Pt Townsend

From Tacoma and southeast

Take State Route (SR) 16 over the Narrows Bridge toward Bremerton. Take State Route (SR) 3 north and follow the signs to the Hood Canal Bridge. Take a left and cross the Hood Canal Bridge. The sign for Port Townsend is about .5 mile ahead. Turn right on State Route (SR) 19 (Beaver Valley Rd.) toward Port Townsend. Continue about 22 miles to the town of Chimacum. Go straight after the 4-way stop on SR 19. 7 miles south of Port Townsend 19 joins with State Route (SR) 20. Follow SR 20 into town. The highway becomes Sims Way, then Water St. and goes past the Keystone-Port Townsend ferry dock
