BirdWanderings

The bird is the word! Namely, Moby Bird, the 1976 Bluebird Wanderlodge which will be our home for several months of sabbatical and recharging.

We left our apartment in Sunnyvale near the end of April 2002, and headed out of the SF Bay Area around May 8th. We're on the last quarter of a big loop around the US. We should be back to California in late April.

We're baaaack! As of May 22nd, 2003. Currently settling into San Jose.
